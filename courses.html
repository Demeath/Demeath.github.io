Courses

If you do not yet have the qualifications or requirements needed for the job you want, consider taking an online course.

Online courses are a great way for you to learn new programming skills, and can get you one step closer to a career in web development especially with us.

These courses can be taken at beginning and intermediate skill levels, and will help you jump straight into programming.

The following online courses are the best at teaching you the necessary for you to become a Front end developer for Hot Beans!

Codecademy is an online learning platform that offers interactive coding courses in various programming languages such as Python, JavaScript, Ruby, and SQL.
Codecademy offers courses for all levels of learning, from beginners who have no prior programming knowledge to advanced learners.

SoloLearn is a mobile and web-based platform that offers free coding courses and a supportive community of learners.
The web development courses on SoloLearn cover a variety of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and more. The platform provides a gamified learning experience, with bite-sized lessons, coding challenges, quizzes, and peer-to-peer support to help learners build practical skills.
SoloLearn's web development courses are available at different levels, from beginner to advanced. The platform provides a flexible learning experience, with self-paced learning and the ability to learn on-the-go via the mobile app.

Udemy is an online learning platform that offers a variety of web development courses for learners of all levels. The courses cover a range of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Python, and more.
Udemy also offers web development certification courses that provide learners with a credential upon completion, demonstrating their proficiency in the field.

Coursera is an online learning platform that partners with universities and other organizations to offer a wide range of courses, including web development..
The web development courses on Coursera cover a variety of topics, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and more. They offer a mix of video lectures, readings, quizzes, and assignments to help learners build practical skills..
